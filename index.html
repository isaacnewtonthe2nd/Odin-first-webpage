Fall season is cozy season

At solace in space, Autumn is the season we make our homes feel warm, cozy, and ready for the holidays.

Fall home decor tips

Create a warm and inviting glow with candles. And they smell great!

Switch out textiles and throw pillows. Colors that mimic autumn leaves are great.

Add more ambient lighting with sconces and portable lamps. These make your space feel warmer.

Roll out some cozy rug underfoot for style and warmth.

"Home, the spot of earth supremely blest, a dearer, sweeter spot than all the rest."
